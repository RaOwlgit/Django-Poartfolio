Hi there,

My name is Rahul Chandrakar, and I'm currently diving headfirst into the world of technology as a third-semester B.Tech student at Rungta College Of Engineering & Technology, Bhilai. I'm thrilled to have you here on my website, a space I've created to share my ongoing journey in the fascinating realm of tech.

Here, you'll find a collection of my projects and creative endeavors, constantly evolving as I delve deeper into new learning resources and content.
